Fairy Tale Sentiment Over Time

By Nina Redpath

Student ID: 458234
Wustl Email: ninaredpath@wustl.edu

This page shows the sentiment of 6 fairy tales over time. The goal is to see if there is any consistent story arc pattern between tales and to compare each stories sentiment to one another. The analysis is done by scoring words in the story and then plotting them according to the time index at which they occur in the fairytale. You can click on the book icon associated with each story to see its sentiment graph, or click "compare all" to see a stacked chart.
